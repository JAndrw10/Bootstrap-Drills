Star Wars characters and info

Luke Skywalker

Originally a farmer on Tatooine living with his uncle and aunt, Luke becomes a pivotal figure in the Rebel Alliance's struggle against the Galatic Empire. The son of fallen Jedi knight Anakin Skywalker, Luke is the twin brother of Rebellion leader Princess leia and eventual brother-in-law of smuggler Han Solo.

Anakin Skywalker

Originally as a slave on Tatooine, Anakin is a Jedi prophesied to become one of the most powerful Jedi ever who would bring balance to the Force. Anakin is later lured to the dark side of the Force by Palpatine and becomes a Sith lord, assuming the title Darth Vadar later serving as the Galatic Empire's chief enforcer.

Master Yoda

Yoda is a mentor to Obi-Wan Kenobi, and lives in exile on the swamp planet of Dagobah. He trains Luke Skywalker in the ways of the Force, later Yoda leads the Jedi High Council and trains the young generations of Jedi until they are assigned to a master.

Princess Leia

Leia is the Princess of the planet Alderaan, a member of the Imperial Senate and an agent of the Rebel Alliance. Leia helps destroy the Empire's superweapon, the Death Star.

Obi-Wan Kenobi

Obi-Wan is a Jedi Master who is a mentor to Luke Skywalker, to whom he introduces the ways of the Jedi. Obi-Wan guides Luke through the Force in his fight against the Galatic Empire.

Rey Skywalker

Rey is introduced as a scavenger who was left behind on the planet of Jakku when she was a child. She later becomes involved with the Resisitance's conflict with the First Order, Rey trains to be a Jedi under Luke Skywalker and General leia.
